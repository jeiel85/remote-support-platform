# Terms of Service (Draft)

**Status: engineering draft pending qualified counsel review and signature.
Not published or binding. Not legal advice.**

## Acceptable use

- The service authorizes remote support sessions only where the remote
  device operator has given explicit, visible, revocable consent before
  view/control/clipboard/file/chat access begins (`AT-FR-CON-*`).
- Customers must not use the service to access a device without the
  authorization of its owner/operator, to distribute malware through file
  transfer, or to circumvent the consent/notification indicators described
  in `00-product/user-flows.md`. Violations are handled under
  `08-operations/runbooks/abuse-response.md` and may result in suspension.
- Unattended access (Goal 14, separate release) requires the customer's own
  local/admin enrollment and MFA/policy acceptance; it is never enabled
  silently through a normal attended session.

## Service description and limitations

- The supported capability matrix (`06-quality/supported-capability-matrix.md`)
  is the authoritative statement of what is supported, limited, preview or
  unsupported; marketing may not exceed it.
- The service does not guarantee availability during a declared SEV-1/SEV-2
  incident beyond the published SLO; see `06-quality/performance-and-slo.md`.

## Customer responsibilities

- Maintain accurate tenant membership/roles and revoke access promptly when
  personnel or device custody changes.
- Configure tenant data retention and export/deletion settings consistent
  with the customer's own legal obligations.
